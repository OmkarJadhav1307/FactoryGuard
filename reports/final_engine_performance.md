## Analysis
To hit the 80% Recall target requested, we successfully increased the engine's sensitivity. 
- The model now captures **nearly 9 out of 10 machine failures** before they occur.
- Out of all "High Risk" alerts generated by the model, approximately **1 in 3.5 alerts** is an actual impending failure. 
- In industrial maintenance, this 1:3 ratio is highly professional because the "Cost of a Missed Failure" ($10,000+) is far higher than the "Cost of a Manual Inspection" ($100).
